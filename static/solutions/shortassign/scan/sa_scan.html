Short Assignment #9: Backward Scans
Due: Wednesday, May 22

This short assignment is very short.

As we saw in lecture, in order to perform a segmented reduction, we need to perform a backward, segmented copy-scan. Describe how to do so. Your description may be in prose, rather than code, but you should include an illustrative example. Of course, the parallel running time should be O(log n), assuming that n ≤ p.

Here's an example of what a backward copy-scan should do:

index       0   1   2   3   4   5   6   7   8

seg         1   0   0   1   0   0   0   1   0
x           5   7   8   3   9   4   5   2   6

result      8   8   8   5   5   5   5   6   6

You may submit a plain text file, a Word file, or a PDF.
